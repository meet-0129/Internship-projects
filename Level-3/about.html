Our Journey

Founded in 1995, Pizza Paradise began as a small family-owned pizzeria with a big dream. Our founder, Antonio Rossi, brought his family's secret recipes from Naples, Italy, to create the perfect pizza experience.

Today, we continue to honor our Italian heritage while embracing modern culinary innovations. Every pizza we serve is made with the same passion and attention to detail that Antonio brought to his first pizzeria.
